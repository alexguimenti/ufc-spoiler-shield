# Privacy Policy — UFC Spoiler Blocker

**Last updated:** March 9, 2026

UFC Spoiler Blocker is a browser extension that modifies the visual appearance of UFC pages on Paramount+ to hide spoiler information.

## Data Collection

This extension does **not** collect, store, transmit, or share any user data. It has no analytics, no tracking, no remote servers, and no network requests.

## Permissions

The extension only runs on `https://www.paramountplus.com/*` and uses no special browser permissions. It modifies the page DOM locally in your browser to hide fight durations, blur spoiler thumbnails, and remove result descriptions.

## Third Parties

No data is shared with third parties. The extension operates entirely on your device.
